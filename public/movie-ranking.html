Rohan's Favorite Movies

A quick top 3

Into the Spiderverse

Into the Spiderverse is a very close hitting movie
that inspires me to look inside and grind to become better.

Baby Driver

Baby Driver is a culmination of my childhood dream of
mastering the ability to drive and using the skill to
eventually do good.

It's also a love movie about finding someone who also appreciates
you for being you.

La La Land

La La Land is a movie that takes you for a ride and just
keeps you waiting for more. Its a movie that makes
you think deeper in life.
